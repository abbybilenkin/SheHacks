About A-Ok



How does A-Ok work?
A-OK helps you request quick aid from local users in a non-emergency situation where police and medical services aren't necessary.


When would I need to use A-Ok?
Anything from your car getting stuck in the snow to needing someone to bring you cold medicine.


Who are we?
We're 6 women who code who built this hack at SheHacks Boston 2018. Fork us at github.com/abbybilenkin/SheHacks :)
