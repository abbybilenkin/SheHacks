About A-Ok



When would I need to use A-Ok?
In a post-disaster situation, when emergency personnel are focusing on people in life-threatening conditions, there are still people who
need help. However, there are also people who were not as badly affected and want to help - they just don't quite know how.
This service will match those two groups together, allowing people to help their neighbors with just the click of a button.


How does A-Ok work?
A-OK helps you request quick aid from local users in a non life-threatening situation where police and medical services
are not as readily available as they would be. You can also sign up to be notified via text message when somebody in your area
is in need of food, water, first aid, or other supplies. If you are able to provide any of those things, you can simply respond yes
and are given that person's location so that you can go and help!
